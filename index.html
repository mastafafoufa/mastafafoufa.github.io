My Projects

Method and system of intelligently providing responses for a user in the user's absence

This patent describes a system that automatically responds to messages on behalf of a user when they are unavailable. It utilizes machine learning to understand the incoming message and generate an appropriate response, such as providing information or acknowledging receipt.

Machine-learning-model based name pronunciation

This patent focuses on improving the pronunciation of user names during virtual conferences. It leverages machine learning models to analyze the attributes of other participants in the conference and determine the most appropriate pronunciation for the user's name within that specific context.

Shared visual content filtering during virtual meetings

This patent addresses the issue of inappropriate or distracting content being shared during virtual meetings. It outlines a method for filtering shared visual content in real-time by analyzing the content and comparing it to the context of the meeting. This helps to maintain a productive and professional meeting environment.
